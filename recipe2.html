Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 100g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  3. Add the cooled quinoa to the bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss everything together and serve chilled.
